Explore Canada: A Journey into Paradise

Culture: Throughout Canada's history, its culture has been influenced firstly by its indigenous cultures, and later by European culture and traditions, mostly by the British and French.

Attraction: Canada is a country in North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean and northward into the Arctic Ocean, making it the world's second-largest country by total area, with the world's longest coastline

Activities: Perhaps the most scenes which come to your mind when you hear Canada's name are famous aspects likeice hockey, maple syrup, and brutally cold winters.
